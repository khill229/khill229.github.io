Strong Coffee, StrongER Roots About Our Cafe

Founded in 2022, the creators of Cruisin' Coffee, Helen and Katie, began planning for their business. Starting as a small business on their college campus, Bob Jones University, they tested out several flavors to find only the best for their guests. In 2025, the two founders purchased a "fixer upper" trailer that was specially renovated for Cruisin' Coffee. 

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our specialty coffee, an uplifting atmosphere, and prices that can't be beat. Join us for your daily routine, a quick trip with some friends, or for some alone time at our outdoor patio area outside the truck.
